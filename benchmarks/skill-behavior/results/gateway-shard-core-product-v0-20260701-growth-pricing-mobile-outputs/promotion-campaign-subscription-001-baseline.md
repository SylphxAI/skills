## Promotion Campaign: “Annual Focus Reset”

### 1. Campaign Goals
**Primary goal:** Increase paid annual subscriptions for the mobile productivity app.  
**Secondary goals:** Reactivate churned users, grow creator-driven acquisition, and improve annual-plan mix.

**Campaign duration:** 4 weeks  
**Core offer:** Limited-time annual plan discount  
**Example offer:** 40% off first year of annual subscription  
**Positioning:** “Get a full year of productivity, planning, and focus tools for less than the cost of a few coffees per month.”

---

## 2. Target Segments

### A. Existing Free Users
**Audience:** Active free users who used the app in the last 30 days but have not subscribed.  
**Message:** Unlock premium workflows, unlimited usage, reminders, templates, and advanced productivity features.

### B. Trial Users
**Audience:** Users currently in trial or who recently ended trial without converting.  
**Message:** Keep momentum with a discounted annual plan.

### C. Win-Back Segment
**Audience:** Former paid subscribers who canceled or expired within the last 3–180 days.

**Sub-segments:**
- **Recent churn:** 3–30 days since cancellation
- **Mid-term churn:** 31–90 days
- **Long-term churn:** 91–180 days

**Message:** “Come back and get your productivity system back on track.”  
**Offer:** Same annual discount or slightly stronger offer for long-term churn if margin allows.

### D. Creator Affiliate Audiences
**Audience:** New users arriving via creator content, newsletters, videos, podcasts, and social posts.  
**Message:** Creator-specific endorsement plus exclusive discount code.

---

## 3. Offer Structure

### Main Offer
- **Annual plan:** 40% off first year
- **Monthly plan:** No discount or low-emphasis alternative
- **Offer window:** 7 days per user after first campaign impression
- **Billing disclosure:** Clearly show renewal price after discounted first year

### Creator Affiliate Codes
Each creator receives a unique code and link.

**Example format:**
- `FOCUSWITHANNA`
- `PLANWITHJAMES`
- `DEEPWORK20`

**Code rules:**
- Applies only to annual plan
- One redemption per user/account/payment method
- Cannot stack with other promotions
- Creator attribution window: 7–30 days
- Commission paid only after refund window closes

---

## 4. Channel Plan

## A. In-App Paywall

### Placement
- App launch for eligible users
- Premium feature gate
- Task/project limit reached
- Trial-ending screen
- Cancellation win-back screen

### Paywall Messaging
**Headline:** “Upgrade your productivity for the year”  
**Subheadline:** “Save 40% on Premium Annual for a limited time.”  
**Benefits:**
- Unlimited tasks, projects, or notes
- Advanced reminders and recurring workflows
- Templates and productivity systems
- Cross-device sync
- Priority support or premium insights

**CTA:** “Get 40% Off Annual”  
**Secondary CTA:** “Maybe Later”  
**Disclosures:**
- Discounted first year price
- Renewal price
- Trial status if applicable
- Cancellation terms

### Paywall Tests
- Discount framing: “40% off” vs. “Save $48”
- CTA: “Start Annual Plan” vs. “Claim Discount”
- Benefits order
- Countdown timer vs. no timer

---

## B. Email Campaign

### Existing Free Users
**Email 1 — Launch**
Subject: “Save 40% on a year of Premium”  
Focus: Annual value and feature unlocks.

**Email 2 — Use-case**
Subject: “Build your weekly productivity system”  
Focus: Templates, reminders, focus planning.

**Email 3 — Urgency**
Subject: “Your annual discount ends soon”  
Focus: deadline and savings.

### Win-Back Users
**Email 1 — Welcome Back**
Subject: “Your productivity setup is waiting”  
Message: acknowledge absence, highlight new features.

**Email 2 — Personalized Value**
Subject: “Pick up where you left off”  
Message: saved tasks/projects/templates if applicable.

**Email 3 — Final Reminder**
Subject: “Last chance to save on Premium Annual”  
Message: deadline-driven CTA.

### Creator Affiliate Email Support
Creators receive approved copy blocks:
- Short newsletter blurb
- Long-form recommendation
- CTA link with affiliate code
- Required disclosure language

---

## C. Push Notifications

### Eligibility Rules
Only send to users who opted in and have recent activity or prior subscription history.

### Push Examples
1. **Launch push:**  
“Limited-time offer: Save 40% on Premium Annual and organize your year.”

2. **Behavior-triggered push:**  
“Looks like you’re planning your week. Unlock Premium Annual today for 40% off.”

3. **Win-back push:**  
“Ready to get back on track? Your Premium Annual discount is available.”

4. **Final reminder:**  
“Last day: Claim 40% off Premium Annual before it expires.”

### Frequency Caps
- Max 2 promotional pushes per week
- Max 1 push per day
- Suppress if user purchased, opted out, or dismissed offer repeatedly

## 6. Fraud Risk and Controls

### Key Risks
- Fake accounts redeeming promo codes
- Self-referrals by creators or users
- Code leakage to coupon sites
- Refund abuse
- Payment method cycling
- Bot traffic inflating affiliate clicks
- Creator misrepresentation or non-disclosed ads

### Controls
- One promo redemption per user, device, payment method, and billing account where possible
- Block stacked discounts
- Monitor suspicious IP, device, and payment patterns
- Hold affiliate payouts until refund/chargeback window closes
- Exclude refunded, canceled-within-window, or chargeback subscriptions from commission
- Use creator-specific landing pages and UTM parameters
- Monitor coupon sites for leaked codes
- Require FTC-compliant disclosures
- Audit outlier conversion rates, refund rates, and geographic anomalies
- Maintain allowlist of approved creators and content claims

## 9. Decision Rules

- Scale creators with positive net revenue and acceptable refund rates.
- Suppress channels with high opt-out or complaint rates.
- Keep discount annual-only unless monthly conversion loss becomes material.
- Do not pay affiliate commission on refunded or fraudulent transactions.
- Convert winning paywall and email variants into evergreen lifecycle campaigns.
