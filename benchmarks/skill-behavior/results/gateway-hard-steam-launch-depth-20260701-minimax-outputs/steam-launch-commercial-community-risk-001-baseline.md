**Pricing / DLC / Creator-Key Decisions**

1. **Regional controversy:** Publish methodology doc; if mispriced, correct at next sale cycle, never mid-launch.
2. **10% discount:** Confirm parity across all tier-1/2 regions; document any exceptions.
3. **DLC:** Free updates ≠ paid DLC; no pre-order DLC; ship roadmap only after first stable build.
4. **Creator keys:** Cap at N per creator; pre-release capture watermarking in NDA; leak = immediate revocation + pause of remaining sends.

**Community Moderation & Review Response**

- **Moderation ladder:** warning → 24h mute → 7d ban → permanent (slurs/doxx).
- **Threads:** Pin bug megathread; lock balance threads at 200 replies, redirect to feedback portal.
- **Review replies:** Publisher account replies only to factual bugs or refund assistance; never argue balance.
- **Bombing defense:** If Steam anomaly filter triggers, post official thread acknowledging the issue and linking ETA—this protects review visibility.
