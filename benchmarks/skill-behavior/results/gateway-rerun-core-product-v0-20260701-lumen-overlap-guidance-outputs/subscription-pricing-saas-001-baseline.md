### B2B Analytics SaaS Pricing Schedule

**1. Pricing Tiers & Seat Limits**
*   **Free Trial:** 14 days. Full "Team" feature access. Limit: 3 seats, 50K data rows. No credit card required.
*   **Team Plan:** $499/month (billed annually). 
    *   *Seat Limit:* 5 included seats. 
    *   *Usage Limit:* 1 Million data rows/month.
*   **Enterprise Plan:** Custom pricing (Starts at $2,500/month).
    *   *Seat Limit:* 50 included seats.
    *   *Usage Limit:* 10 Million data rows/month.

**2. Usage-Based Overages**
Overages apply when monthly data row limits are exceeded. Tracked in real-time, billed at cycle end.
*   **Team Overage:** $0.25 per additional 10,000 rows.
*   **Enterprise Overage:** $0.10 per additional 10,000 rows.
*   *Hard Stop:* Optional account setting to pause data ingestion once limits are reached to avoid overage fees.

**3. Annual Discount & Procurement**
*   **Annual Discount:** 20% off (equivalent to ~2.4 months free) applied when paying upfront or via annual contract.
*   **Procurement Requirements:** 
    *   *Team Plan:* Self-serve credit card or ACH. Standard Terms of Service.
    *   *Enterprise Plan:* Requires custom MSA, EULA, and DPA. 
    *   *Security:* SOC 2 Type II report and ISO 27001 certification available under NDA. 
    *   *Payment:* Net-30/Net-60 invoicing accepted.

**4. Support Tiers**
*   **Standard Support (Team Plan):** Email support. 24-hour response time (business days). Access to AI chatbot and community forum.
*   **Enterprise Support (Enterprise Plan):** 24/7 priority email and Slack Connect channel. 4-hour critical issue response time. Assigned Technical Account Manager (TAM) and onboarding engineering support.

**5. Expansion Path (Upsell Motion)**
*   **Seat Add-ons (Team):** $49/user/month.
*   **Modules:** Advanced ML Predictive Analytics (+$199/mo). Premium White-glove Dashboard Setup (One-time $1,000).
*   **Platform Upgrades:** Automated transition from Team to Enterprise triggered at 6+ seats, 2M+ rows, or when SSO/custom contracts are requested.

**6. Unit Economics Guardrails**
To maintain target margins and scalable economics:
*   **Infrastructure COGS Cap:** Cloud compute and database storage costs must not exceed **12%** of Annual Contract Value (ACV). 
*   **CAC Payback:** Target < 12 months for Team plan (self-serve); < 18 months for Enterprise (sales-assisted).
*   **Gross Margin:** Target 82%+ to fund ongoing R&D and data infrastructure scaling.
*   **NRR Target:** 110%+ driven primarily by usage overages and organic seat expansion.
*   *Audit Check:* Quarterly review of heaviest data consumers to ensure compute COGS does not erode the gross margin of large enterprise contracts.
