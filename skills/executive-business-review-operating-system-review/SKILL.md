# Executive Business Review Operating System Review

Use this skill to convert executive business review, QBR/EBR cadence, strategic account value reviews, executive sponsor alignment, renewal risk, expansion planning, adoption narrative, and action-register questions into a concrete artifact with owners, gates, metrics, and recovery paths.

## Workflow

1. Identify account tier, customer goals, executive audience, sponsor map, usage and ROI evidence, renewal window, expansion hypothesis, open risks, roadmap asks, support history, and decision owners.
2. Read `references/executive-business-review-operating-system-patterns.md`.
3. Classify the situation as first executive review, quarterly business review, renewal-risk review, expansion review, implementation recovery review, sponsor-change review, or strategic roadmap alignment review.
4. Define review cadence, prep workflow, agenda, value evidence, adoption metrics, risk register, roadmap ask handling, action owners, follow-up rhythm, and renewal/expansion signal capture.
5. Produce EBR operating system, state machine, decision table, event schema, agenda template, action register, sponsor map, and follow-up plan.

## When not to use

- Do not use for generic advice the base model already handles without this skill's specific artifact contract.

## Guardrails

- Do not make EBRs a generic status deck; tie every section to customer outcomes, decisions, risks, or executive action.
- Do not let sales expansion goals override unresolved adoption, support, security, or implementation risks.
- Do not promise roadmap items without owner, decision status, and commitment boundary.
- Do not close an EBR without written actions, owners, dates, and next review trigger.

## Output format

```text
Executive review context:
Audience / source of truth / risk boundary:

EBR operating plan:

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Cadence, agenda, value proof, risks, expansion signals, actions, and follow-up:
- <trigger> -> <policy, metric, edge case, support note>
```
