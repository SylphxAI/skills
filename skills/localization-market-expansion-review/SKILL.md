# Localization Market Expansion Review

Use this skill to convert a high-stakes product decision into a concrete, measurable, reviewable operating artifact.

## Workflow

1. Identify target market, language, customer segment, channel, local competitors, revenue model, and support capacity.
2. Read `references/localization-expansion-patterns.md`.
3. Separate product internationalization, translation, pricing/payment, legal/compliance, support, store/listing, and marketing needs.
4. Define market-entry sequence: research, smoke test, localization depth, launch channel, support loop, and post-launch learning.
5. Produce readiness matrix, localization scope, risk register, event plan, and rollout milestones.

## When not to use

- Do not use for generic advice the base model already handles without this skill's specific artifact contract.

## Guardrails

- Do not equate translation with market readiness.
- Do not localize pricing, claims, regulated content, or legal terms without qualified review.
- Avoid launching paid acquisition before support, refund, and payment paths are ready.
- Preserve locale-specific formatting, accessibility, and right-to-left or text-expansion needs.
