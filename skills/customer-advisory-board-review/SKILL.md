# Customer Advisory Board Review

Use this skill to convert a customer advisory boards, design partners, roadmap signal, member selection, agendas, commitments, and feedback loops question into a concrete artifact with owners, gates, metrics, and recovery paths.

## Workflow

1. Identify target customer segment, decision questions, member criteria, influence boundaries, confidentiality needs, incentives, cadence, and owner.
2. Read `references/customer-advisory-board-patterns.md`.
3. Classify format: advisory board, design partner group, beta cohort, creator council, executive council, research panel, or community roundtable.
4. Define selection criteria, agenda, discussion prompts, evidence capture, commitment policy, follow-up, roadmap linkage, and member health checks.
5. Produce CAB plan, state machine, decision table, event schema, facilitation checklist, and anti-bias guardrails.

## Guardrails

- Do not let the loudest or highest-revenue customer become the whole market.
- Do not promise roadmap delivery, exclusivity, or discounts unless owners explicitly approve.
- Do not collect sensitive competitive/customer data without confidentiality and consent boundaries.
- Do not run advisory meetings without synthesis, decisions, and follow-up back to participants.

## Output format

```text
Segment / decision question / member influence:

Advisory board plan:

Members, agenda, and evidence:
- <item> -> <policy, metric, edge case, support note>

- <trigger> -> <action, communication, owner>
```
