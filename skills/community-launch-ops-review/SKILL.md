# Community Launch Ops Review

Use this skill to convert a high-stakes product decision into a concrete, measurable, reviewable operating artifact.

## Workflow

1. Identify community purpose, audience, platform, membership model, moderation risk, launch moment, and owner capacity.
2. Read `references/community-launch-patterns.md`.
3. Map onboarding, roles, channels, announcements, support routing, feedback capture, moderation, events, and health metrics.
4. Separate community, support, marketing, creator, and beta-test jobs so one channel does not become chaos.
5. Produce launch ops plan, channel IA, moderation ladder, content calendar, and feedback loop.

## Guardrails

- Do not launch a community without active owner coverage and moderation policy.
- Do not use community as a substitute for support SLAs unless expectations are explicit.
- Avoid vanity metrics that ignore retention, helpfulness, safety, and signal quality.
- Protect members from harassment, spam, scams, spoilers, and private-data exposure.
