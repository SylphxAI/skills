# PC Store Launch Assets

Use this module for a Steam or other PC-store media pack. Store Listing owns
narrative, metadata, selection and order; Marketing owns audience/message and
campaign brief; Distribution owns upload/review/release proof. This module owns
the exact visual/audio files and their truth, rights and QA evidence.

## Promise-to-file audit

For every selected capsule/key-art, screenshot, trailer, demo clip, thumbnail,
creator/press file and localized derivative, record:

```text
exact build/branch/scene/save/seed and capture script:
asset role, audience, placement and owning brief/request revision:
genre/core-loop claim and supporting product state:
shown OS/hardware/input/controller/settings/language/accessibility state:
demo versus final-product scope and DLC/base-product boundary:
locale, aspect/safe zone, captions/transcript/audio description/alt text:
rights/talent/music/UGC/synthetic-media records:
technical, claim, visual, accessibility and locale QA plus byte digest:
```

## Rules

- `pc-asset-1` — Capsule, trailer, screenshots and thumbnail present the same
  genre/core-loop promise and named audience; visual excitement cannot invent a
  mechanic, mode, fidelity tier or availability.
- `pc-asset-2` — Show real product interaction early in a gameplay/workflow
  trailer unless the approved genre narrative explicitly justifies another
  opening; preserve a truthful first-frame and thumbnail.
- `pc-asset-3` — Screenshots and footage bind exact build, UI state, hardware/
  quality tier, input glyphs, locale and online/offline/server condition.
- `pc-asset-4` — Demo/playtest media states its scope and cannot imply final
  features, DLC, multiplayer, saves, achievements, controller, language or
  performance support that exact build evidence does not prove.
- `pc-asset-5` — Creator/press kits include exact build/version, approved
  description/claims, known-issues note, rights/credit/disclosure terms, contact
  and supersession route; they do not silently outlive a corrected build.
- `pc-asset-6` — Produce selected aspect/locale/accessibility variants from
  owned masters; do not crop away critical UI, burn inaccessible text into
  video, or reuse one controller/locale state universally.

## Acceptance

Cross-check the pack against Store Listing claim/order requests, Marketing
briefs, Distribution build and availability evidence, and Launch Readiness
known issues. Reject mismatch in core loop, demo scope, settings, language,
controller/input, achievements/cloud, system requirements, price/DLC wording,
release timing, rights or accessibility. A locally valid file is not proof that
the storefront published it; hand exact digests downstream for that readback.
