# Changes, Renewals, and System Handoffs

## Deviation record

```text
deviation_id, baseline_clause_version, proposed_text_or_business_change,
requester_and_reason, legal_commercial_operational_effects, risk_and_exposure,
fallback_positions, downstream_system_changes, precedent_scope,
approvers_and_authority, approval_expiry, final_source, review_or_cleanup_date
```

## Calendar decision table

| Event | Required truth | Unsafe shortcut |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Renewal approaching | exact term, notice rule/date/time zone, current status, authority | CRM close date |
| Price/usage change | contractual right, notice method/timing, pricing approval, billing readiness | generic email campaign |
| True-up | meter definition, period, exclusions, evidence, dispute path | unverified usage dashboard |
| Co-term/expansion | amendment/order authority, entitlement/billing effective dates | provisioning from sales note |
| Downgrade/cancellation | notice authority, term effect, data/access/support consequences | immediate service removal |
| Service credit | qualifying event, calculation, claim/automatic rule, approval, ledger handoff | unsupported goodwill amount |
| Termination | grounds/authority, notice proof, effective date, data/entitlement/finance path | automated irreversible action |

## Handoff contract

Every operational handoff contains obligation/term ID, exact source/version, approved normalized instruction, effective/expiry time, account/product scope, expected target state, owner/authority, idempotency key, evidence callback, failure/escalation, correction/reversal, and customer communication owner.

Reconcile at minimum:

- price/discount/currency/tax/payment schedule to quote, billing, invoice, and finance;
- product/seat/usage/service entitlements to provisioning and meters;
- SLA/support/reporting/implementation obligations to operating owners and evidence;
- security/privacy/residency/subprocessor terms to approved assurance and product boundaries;
- renewal, notice, price change, true-up, cancellation, termination, and exception clocks to observed delivery/decision proof;
- custom product/roadmap commitments to explicit product authority or a blocked state.

## Renewal evidence pack

Include exact sources and amendments; term/notice calendar; open obligations and evidence; service credits/disputes; pricing/usage/true-up truth; billing/entitlement reconciliation; security/privacy/support commitments; product/implementation gaps; customer outcomes from the canonical success owner; approved options; decisions/owners/dates; communication and signature status. Do not turn the pack into legal interpretation or a pressure tactic.
