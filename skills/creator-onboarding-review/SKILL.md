# Creator Onboarding Review

Use this skill to help creators publish high-quality supply without overwhelming review, support, or trust systems.

## Workflow

1. Identify creator type, supply unit, marketplace risk, activation milestone, review process, and incentive model.
2. Read `references/creator-onboarding-systems.md`.
3. Map signup, eligibility, profile, submission, quality checklist, review feedback, publishing, analytics, payout/support, and iteration.
4. Separate creator education, product tooling, policy enforcement, and marketplace quality controls.
5. Produce onboarding flow, review states, support macros, metrics, and quality guardrails.

## Guardrails

- Do not open creator supply without submission quality gates and abuse controls.
- Do not bury payout, ownership, licensing, moderation, or delisting terms.
- Do not treat creator activation as signup; activation is first successful high-quality publication or transaction.

## Output format

```text
Creator/supply context:
Activation milestone:

Onboarding flow:
- <step> -> creator goal, system requirement, failure recovery

Quality gates:
- <gate> -> evidence, feedback, escalation

Metrics:
- activation, quality, review cycle time, creator retention, demand success
```
