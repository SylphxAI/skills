# GEO Visibility Review

Use this skill to optimize pages for citation in AI-generated answers, not just ranking in traditional SERPs.

## Workflow

1. Define the target URL, audience, category, and business goal.
2. Read `references/geo-visibility-patterns.md`.
3. Audit technical access: robots.txt, sitemap, meta tags, schema, page speed, and AI crawler allowance.
4. Score content structure for answer extraction: headings, lists, tables, citations, statistics, and FAQ coverage.
5. Apply GEO methods with the highest evidence-backed lift.
6. Produce a prioritized remediation plan with validation steps and refresh cadence.

## Guardrails

- Do not recommend keyword stuffing; it reduces AI visibility.
- Do not invent statistics, quotes, or third-party citations.
- Do not promise ranking or citation outcomes without measurement plan.
- Separate traditional SEO fixes from GEO-specific structure changes.
