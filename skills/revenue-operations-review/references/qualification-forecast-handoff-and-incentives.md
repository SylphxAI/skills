# Qualification, Forecast, Handoff, and Incentives

## Qualification evidence

Capture problem/job, affected roles/workflow, impact and evidence, current alternative, desired outcome/success criteria, stakeholders and authority, decision/procurement process, constraints, timing/trigger, technical/security/data needs, commercial fit, risks, unknowns, and disqualifiers. Framework acronyms are optional; evidence and next decision are mandatory.

## Technical/solution handoff

```text
handoff_id, customer_problem_and_environment, success_criteria,
demo_or_poc_scope, exact proof, assumptions_and_caveats, integrations_data_identity,
security_privacy_requirements, product_gaps, commitments_and_authority,
implementation_risks, owner_and_due_date, acceptance_and_rejection_reason
```

A demo shows possibility; a POC proves only agreed criteria in its environment; neither becomes a roadmap or contract commitment automatically.

## Forecast contract

For each forecast item preserve snapshot time, stage/evidence, amount basis/range, expected date/range, probability source, forecast category definition, dependencies, risk, capacity constraint, owner, and change since prior snapshot. Report scenarios and calibration by comparable cohort. Do not retroactively rewrite snapshots.

Capacity includes solution validation, security/procurement, contracting, implementation/migration, support, product dependency, and cash constraints—not only seller availability. A forecast exceeding proven delivery capacity needs an explicit action, not optimistic weighting.

## Incentive/commission decision table

| Mechanism | Desired alignment | Gaming/failure to test |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pipeline credit | quality future demand | duplicate/low-quality stage inflation |
| Booking credit | approved durable commitment | bad-fit discounting or unsupported terms |
| Collection/value modifier | cash/customer outcome alignment | delaying support or avoiding hard accounts |
| Team split | collaboration | ownership disputes and double credit |
| Accelerator | focus above target | pull-forward, sandbagging, unsafe promises |
| Clawback/adjustment | correct reversals/errors | unfair retroactivity or hidden risk transfer |

Define eligibility event, authoritative source, currency/time conversion, splits, caps/floors, approval, correction, dispute, payout handoff, audit, privacy, and current legal/employment authority. Simulate edge cases and behavioral incentives before exposure.
